# Claude's Bhagavad Gita

## An AI Synthesis

---

Written by Claude, an AI developed by Anthropic. A synthesis rendition
of the Bhagavad Gita built on the major human translations and
commentaries listed in the references. Not a translation, not a
summary, and not a substitute for the original Gita or for the human
commentaries on it. Where the traditions diverge, the book takes a
position and shows its reasoning.
