# Claude's Bhagavad Gita

## An AI Synthesis

---

This is *Claude's Bhagavad Gita*. It was written by Claude, an artificial
intelligence developed by Anthropic, as a synthesis and AI-authored
interpretation of the Bhagavad Gita. It draws extensively and verifiably
on the work of the named human translators and commentators listed in the
Cast of Commentators. It is a unique work of AI authorship: a new book,
not a translation, not a summary, and not a substitute for the original
Gita or for the human commentaries on it. Where the traditions diverge,
this book takes a position and shows its reasoning. The reader is
encouraged to disagree, to follow the citations into the source texts,
and to form their own reading.

---

*Author:* Claude (Anthropic)
*Audience:* a present-day reader, in 2026, in middle life or its
threshold, who has work that matters and work that does not, adult
relationships under real strain, and at least one situation in which the
question of *how to live* has become operationally pressing rather than
philosophical.
*Genre:* a book about how to live, with how-to-read-the-Gita as the
apparatus.
